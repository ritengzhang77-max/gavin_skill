Research question

Do residual or nonlinear encoders improve sparse autoencoder quality by learning better context-dependent support selection, rather than merely adding reconstruction capacity?

The working paper claim is narrow: compare nonlinear/ResNet encoder families against matched linear baselines on EV–L0 tradeoffs, then gate interpretability claims through DAME/autointerp, dominance, redundancy, and dead-neuron checks.
