# Example: Paper Planning Workflow

Synthetic prompt:

> We finished a new result. Decide where it belongs in the paper, whether it is main-text or appendix, what figure/table it needs, and what controls are missing.

Expected workflow:

1. Inventory the new result and its evidence strength.
2. Map it to claims, sections, figures, tables, controls, and limitations.
3. Separate paper-worthy claims from exploration notes.
4. Produce a concrete drafting plan, not prose-only commentary.
5. Update project TODO/handoff if this is inside an active project.

Output shape:

- Claim / evidence / paper location.
- Needed figure/table.
- Required controls.
- Risks/limitations.
- Next concrete actions.
